***************************
Dome subjected to settlement
***************************

.. _example-dome-2:

This example shows a hemispheric dome subjected to a split displacement. This problem has been discussed on the publication:

* R. Maia Avelino, A. Iannuzzo, T. Van Mele, and P. Block, ‘An energy-based strategy to find admissible thrust networks compatible with foundation settlements in masonry structures’, Mechanics Research Communications, vol. 125, p. 103978, Oct. 2022, doi: `10.1016/j.mechrescom.2022.103978 <https://doi.org/10.1016/j.mechrescom.2022.103978>`_.
